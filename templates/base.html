Why is Depression Detection important?


  1. 01

    Raising depression awareness can help defeat the stigma of the illness and can also help people suffering from it to reach out and get the help that they need to defeat it.

  2. 02

    Early detection, intervention, and appropriate treatment can promote remission, prevent relapse, and reduce the emotional and financial burden of the disease.

  3. 03

    There are at least a dozen advantages to obtaining an early and accurate diagnosis when cognitive symptoms are first noticed. These advantages result in a higher quality of life, less stress for family care partners, more time to treasure the present, and increased time for future planning.

  4. 04

    Diagnosis is the process of finding out if a patient has a specific disease. A medical professional prescribes a test to make a diagnosis or to exclude possible illness.

Things you could do to fight
Depression .

Medications

There are various medications and medical procedures to treat depression and anxiety.

Consult for therapy

Therapies like yoga, meditation, and acupuncture can keep symptoms of depression at bay by regulating mood.

Reach out to Support Groups

Antidepressants can be helpful on their own, but combining medication and therapy is much more effective for managing depression in the long run.

EEG For Depression Detection


Electroencephalography, or EEG, technology that measures brain function could encourage earlier diagnoses of common mental and neurological disorders, including Depression, autism, ADHD and dementia.
